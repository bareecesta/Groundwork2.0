About

Groundwork is a social enterprise barista training company using coffee as a pathway into work. We equip young adults with practical skills, build their confidence and connect them with meaningful hospitality roles.

Our mission: to create routes into meaningful hospitality work for young people through barista training, mentoring and employer partnerships.

We work with young adults who are in school, leaving education or currently not in work, giving them skills that cafés actually need.

What we do

Our programmes build up from coffee basics to real-world readiness, so every young person leaves with confidence and employable skills.

Barista Fundamentals

Espresso, milk texturing and station workflow so trainees can jump onto the machine with confidence.

Latte Art & Service Skills

Customer-facing training covering speed, presentation and creating the warm welcome cafés rely on.

Employer-Ready

Soft skills, punctuality and workplace expectations to make sure trainees succeed on placement and beyond.
